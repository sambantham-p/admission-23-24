THE INSTITUTE

PIONEER IN TECHNICAL EDUCATION

An initiative by the rich legacy of PSG & Sons' Charities with a great vision of it's founding father "Let there be charity, so that others can share my family's prosperity." The institution is ranked among the top 100 in the country.

PSG College of Technology, is Govt. Aided, Autonomous, Affiliated to Anna University and ISO 9001:2015 certified Institution. This is one of the foremost institutions founded by the PSG & Sons' Charities Trust (1926). The College was established in the year 1951. PSG College of Technology is currently accredited by NAAC, most of its courses accredited by NBA and Ranked 53 by NIRF - 2022

he All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE) has accorded approval to admit the students under Foreign Nationals/ Overseas Citizen of India / Children of Indian Workers in Gulf Countries categories.
